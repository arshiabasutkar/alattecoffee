Our Purpose

Welcome to "A Latte Coffee"! Get it? Like, a lotta coffee? Ah, we just love coffee so much that we decided to create a website all about it. With this website, we hope to place emphasis on smaller, local businesses that struggle to profit with major coffee chains such as Dunkin' Donuts and Starbucks in the way. You, the user, also have the opportunity to explore coffee shops in your area and, if you can't go to a coffee shop, create your own coffee with our recipes and YouTube videos. We hope you enjoy!
