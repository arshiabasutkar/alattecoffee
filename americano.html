How to Make an Americano

Ingredients

  • espresso machine
  • hot/boiling water

Instructions

  1. Pull a 3 oz espresso shot into a separate glass.
  2. Pour about 3 oz of hot water into the mug you plan to drink from.
  3. Pour the espresso shot into the mug.
  4. Serve.

Congratulations! You have made an americano!
