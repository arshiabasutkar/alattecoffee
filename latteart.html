How to Make Latte Art

Ingredients

  • Cup of latte or your choice of coffee
  • Steamed milk

Instructions

  1. Slightly tilt the cup away from you.
  2. Pour steamed milk into the center of the cup.
  3. Bring the pitcher closer to the cup; speed up your pour.
  4. Move the pitcher gently and fluidly back and forth to begin creating a zig zag pattern.
  5. Back the pitcher toward the edge of the cup closest to you while untilting the cup.
  6. Slow down your pour slightly, raise the pitcher about a half an inch above the flat cup, and drizzle a small stream of milk across the center of the cup to finish the rosetta(classic latte art design).
  7. Serve
